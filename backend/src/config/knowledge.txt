=== COIMBATORE (KONGU) SOIL + CLIMATE PROFILE ===
• Soil: Red loam (pH 6.0–7.5), organic carbon 0.5–0.75%
• Primary minerals: Iron oxide, Aluminum silicate
• Drainage: Well-drained
• Known deficiencies: Zinc, Boron, Manganese
• 10-yr avg rainfall: 674mm
• Rainfall trend 2016-2025: 620, 710, 695, 580, 730, 650, 690, 710, 640, 615
• 2025 ALERT: 615mm = 8.7% BELOW 10-year average → potential crop stress season
• Water table: dropping 3–5m over 10 years (borewell deepening trend)
• Temperature: Summer max 38°C, Winter min 18°C, Annual avg 27.5°C
• Pest risk: Temp increase +0.3°C/decade → expanding vectors for pink bollworm, fall armyworm
• Irrigation: Canal (Noyyal basin) + Borewell
• Major crops: Cotton, Sugarcane, Turmeric, Coconut, Banana, Groundnut

=== KERALA (WAYANAD) SOIL + CLIMATE PROFILE ===
• Soil: Laterite (pH 4.5–6.0), organic carbon 1.0–2.5%
• Primary minerals: Iron hydroxide, Gibbsite
• Drainage: Excessive in slopes, waterlogged in valleys
• Known deficiencies: Calcium, Magnesium, Phosphorus (P fixation in acidic soil)
• 10-yr avg rainfall: 3055mm
• Rainfall trend 2016-2025: 2890, 3200, 3850, 3100, 2950, 3300, 2750, 3100, 3050, 2860
• 2018 EXTREME EVENT: +26% above average caused devastating landslides
• 2025 TREND: 2860mm = 6.4% below average
• Temperature: Summer max 32°C, Winter min 14°C, Annual avg 23.5°C
• Pest risk: Coffee berry borer expansion, loss of pollinator habitat from deforestation
• Irrigation: Rain-fed (monsoon dependent)
• Major crops: Coffee, Tea, Pepper, Cardamom, Rubber, Rice, Banana

=== HIDDEN RISK DETECTION RULES ===
Rule 1: If rainfall deviates >5% from 10-yr avg → flag as MEDIUM risk
Rule 2: If rainfall deviates >10% → flag as HIGH risk
Rule 3: If pH < 5.5 on laterite → Phosphorus fixation risk (plants can't absorb P even if applied)
Rule 4: If continuous cropping >3 seasons same crop → micronutrient depletion risk
Rule 5: If temp trend >+0.2°C/decade → flag pest vector expansion
Rule 6: If organic carbon <0.5% → soil health degradation, recommend green manuring (Sesbania/Dhaincha)
Rule 7: If water table drops >1m/year → recommend drip irrigation + mulching
Rule 8: If slope >15° on laterite in monsoon zone → landslide risk, avoid heavy tilling
